Phase 1 — LinkedIn Pre-Warm
Day 0
LinkedIn View their profile
Visit the lead's LinkedIn profile. They get a notification that someone viewed them. Creates name recognition before any outreach.
Day 1
LinkedIn Send connection request
Short, no-pitch note: "Hi [Name], I work with [industry] companies in UAE — would love to connect."
Batch: 25 contacts/day → 250 contacts = 10 days to send all.
Day 2–10 wait
Passive Continue sending batches + wait for accepts
Each day: send next batch of 25 connection requests.
Meanwhile, earlier batches start getting accepted/declined. Update Sheet daily.
⏲ No action needed yet — just track accepts in the Sheet (LI Status = accepted / pending)
Phase 2 — Email Sequence (SmartLead)
Day 11
Email Auto Email 1 — Opening email
SmartLead sends Email 1 to ALL 250 contacts.
LI accept/decline does NOT matter — everyone gets the email.
◆ Check after 24–48h
✔ Email replied → STOP LinkedIn for this lead. Reply in email. Mark "replied" in Sheet.
✘ Email bounced → Jump to Bounce Recovery (see below)
Day 14
Email Auto Email 2 — Follow-up
SmartLead auto-sends Email 2 to everyone who hasn't replied or bounced.
◆ Check after 24h
✔ Email replied → STOP LinkedIn. Reply in email only.
→ No reply → Proceed to Day 15 LinkedIn Nudge check
Decision Point — LinkedIn Nudge
Day 15 manual
LinkedIn LinkedIn Nudge — conditional
Rita checks the Sheet for leads that are past Email 2 with no reply. For each one:
◆ Two conditions must BOTH be true
✔ LI accepted + No email reply → Send ONE LinkedIn DM: "Hey [Name], sent you a quick note via email about [topic] — curious if it's on your radar?"
— LI NOT accepted + No email reply → Do nothing. Can't DM someone who didn't accept. Let Email 3 handle it.
— LI accepted + Already replied to email → Do nothing on LinkedIn. Conversation is already happening in email.
◆ After sending LinkedIn nudge — wait 48h
✔ They reply on LinkedIn → STAY on LinkedIn. Pause SmartLead sequence for this contact. Mark "replied" in Sheet.
→ They reply to Email (not LI) → STAY in email. Stop LinkedIn. They chose their channel.
— No reply anywhere → Let Email 3 (breakup) fire on Day 18. No more LinkedIn.
Day 18
Email Auto Email 3 — Breakup email
Final email in the SmartLead sequence. Sent only to leads with no reply to Email 1 or 2.
◆ Final check after 72h
✔ They reply → Continue in email. This is a warm lead now.
✘ No reply → Archive. Dead lead for now. Revisit in 60–90 days.
Phase 3 — Resolution
Day 21 review
Final Review & Archive
Every lead ends up in one of these states. Update the Sheet.
★ Replied (email or LinkedIn) Active conversation → move to "Engaged" tab. Continue in whichever channel they replied on.
★ Booked a call Move to "Booked" tab. Prepare for strategy session.
🗐 No reply on any channel Archive → "Dormant" tab. Set reminder to revisit in 60–90 days with a fresh angle.
✘ Unsubscribed / asked to stop Remove from all lists permanently. Do NOT contact again.
Bounce Recovery Path
When Email 1 bounces — what to do based on LinkedIn status

● Bounced + LI Accepted

→ LinkedIn becomes your PRIMARY channel
→ Send LinkedIn DM with your pitch (don't wait for Day 15)
→ Remove from SmartLead sequence (no point sending to bad email)
→ Follow up on LinkedIn at Day 18 if no LI reply

● Bounced + LI NOT Accepted

✘ Dead lead — no way to reach them
✘ Remove from SmartLead
… Optional: try to find alternate email via Apollo/Google
… If no alt email found → archive permanently

Rita's Daily Routine
10 minutes every morning before starting outreach. Same order every day.

1. Check SmartLead ~3 min

Open SmartLead → check for new replies
For each reply: update Sheet → Email Status = "replied"
Check for bounces → mark "bounced" in Sheet
Reply to warm leads in SmartLead (don't touch them on LI)

2. Check LinkedIn ~3 min

Check for new connection accepts → update LI Status in Sheet
Check for DM replies → update Sheet, pause SmartLead for those contacts
Reply to LI conversations (don't start email for them)

3. Sync Decisions ~2 min

Filter Sheet: LI Accepted = yes + Email Status = no reply + past Email 2
Send LinkedIn nudge DMs to those contacts (max 1 nudge per contact ever)
Filter: Email bounced + LI Accepted → send pitch DM on LinkedIn

4. New Outreach ~2 min

Send today's batch of 25 LI connection requests (if still in Phase 1)
Log which batch was sent today in Sheet
SmartLead handles email sending automatically — no manual action needed
